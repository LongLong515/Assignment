Character Guides

Master your favorite Fragpunk characters with our comprehensive guides.

Broker

Broker is a high-damage, explosive-focused Lancer who is AN ABSOLUTE BEAST at area denial, vision control, and trap placement. His rocket launcher and smoke screens make him a strong aggressive playmaker, while his trap bombs can force enemies out of cover. If you like blowing things up and controlling the battlefield, Broker is a solid (aka the best) choice.

  • Q: Goddard's Vengeance
  • E: Smokestack Lightning
  • T: Cherry Bomb

Nitro

Nitro is a beginner-friendly Lancer in Fragpunk with a relatively straightforward kit. She has a deployable drone that can be used to scout safely around bomb sites, distract the enemies while your teammates push, and, if your aim is particularly good, even snag a couple of kills.

  • Q: Chug Chug
  • E: The Wall
  • T: Mr.PewPew
Full Guide

Jaguar

Jaguar is one of the trickier Lancers to use in Fragpunk, but his overall utility is still useful even if you're still learning how to use his various abilities. His Live Wire trap deals damage and slows enemies it ensnares, his trap cage highlights enemies within its boundaries who are on low HP or affected by his trap, and his detection utility allows you to see how many enemies are in front of Jaguar or on low HP.

  • Q: Live Wire
  • E: Electric Avenue
  • T: I Can See For Miles
